The file 'GerberFiles_v1.5.rar' contains the generated gerber files and was sent to JLCPCB to manufacture the board.

Files 'bom.xlsx' and 'top.xlsx' were written to JLCPCB's specs to make use of their assembly service for the top side of the board, so it came with the FPC connector already mounted. The remaining components on the bottom side were assembled later
